## Whoami
I'm from Venezuela 🇻🇪, born in the '96. Since I was a child I'm all
about problem solving, breaking things and fixing them 🔧 — sometimes
to learn something new, and sometimes to turn them into something else
— to be honest, my mom was never _too_ happy about the latter 🤷‍♂️. 
Anyhow, I have a degree which screams that I should spend the rest of
my life in front of spreadsheets, but soon enough I discovered that's not 
my cup of tea.
### Self taught 👨‍💻
During the 2020's I decided to dive into the programming world. So I 
learned the fundamentals, played around with Python, then learned
about Linux 🐧 and jumped straight into the rabbit hole, going from 
"Why I have to use a terminal?" to somehow deploying services and apps
on my home network across different machines (with different 
architectures too).
### Full stack - Coming soon 🚀 
I remain in ❤ with the Web development world, so I'm constantly 
learning everything I can about it and enjoy every second of it. It's 
just something about the cycle of `Think ➡ Desing ➡ Create ➡ Test ➡
Build ➡ Deploy` which I'm really into.
